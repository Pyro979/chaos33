33 Seconds of Chaos?

On your turn, you'll get a Word and a Chaos Prompt that tells you how to give clues. You have 33 seconds to get someone to guess the word!

Sometimes you'll trigger a Duel instead: a head-to-head challenge for 2 points.

Pass the device to the next player after each turn.
