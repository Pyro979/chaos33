33 Seconds of Chaos, Pass and Play App Rules

Can you get everyone to guess 'Banjo' in a Movie Announcer voice? Or while doing the Macarena? Clock's ticking, everyone's shouting. Then a head-to-head Duel or a Mass Duel with the whole table. 33 Seconds of Chaos: The social party game where the cards make it weird. One device. Pass it around.

Quick Start

  • Tap Start!, pick how many players (or ∞ for free play), then the first turn begins.
  • You get a Word turn, a Duel, or sometimes a Mass Duel.
  • When you're done, tap Next player and pass the device.

Word Turn

  1. Read the Chaos Prompt out loud.
  2. Keep the Word secret.
  3. Tap the timer to start 33 seconds.
  4. Give clues that follow the prompt and the Basic Rules.
  5. Everyone else shouts unlimited guesses.

When someone guesses the word:

  • Stop the timer if it's still running.
  • Reveal the word.
  • Tap Next Player.

If time runs out:

  • Reveal the word.
  • Tap Next Player.

Close enough counts. You decide if a guess counts. If it's close, give it to them and keep moving.

Duel

  1. The screen tells you who duels.
  2. Choose a Neutral Judge (someone not dueling).
  3. The Neutral Judge reads the duel out loud and runs it.
  4. Duel until the Neutral Judge declares a winner.
  5. Tap Next Player.

Judge's call is final.

Some duels (Alpha Blitz, Theme Blitz, Scavenge) hide key info until you tap Tap to reveal so nobody gets a head start.

Mass Duel

  1. Read the card. Everyone plays.
  2. Use Tap to reveal when it shows, same as Duels.
  3. Follow the card. Judge or vote if you need a winner.
  4. Winner gets 2 points (same as a Duel).
  5. Tap Next Player.

Swap

If a prompt, word, or duel is a bad fit, tap Swap and confirm. Mass Duels can't be swapped in the app. Agree at the table if you want a redo.

Basic Rules

Unless the prompt says otherwise, you may not:

  • Say any part of the word (including obvious root forms or plurals).
  • Spell it, write it, or trace letters.
  • Say "rhymes with."
  • Show the object or an image of it.

Any player can call a rule break. If it's unclear, vote fast, then move on.

Optional Scoring

If you want a winner, track points however you like.

Points

  • Word turn (guessed in time): clue-giver +1, first correct guesser +1
  • Word turn (no guess): nobody scores
  • Duel or Mass Duel: winner +2

Rounds

Play 3 rounds (each player takes one turn per round). High score wins.

Ties

Run one more duel. Winner takes it.

Pass & Play - 2 players

With two people, every turn is a Duel. Word turns and Mass Duels are off. You’ll play 6 duels (3 rounds × 2 players).

Quick start

  • After you pick 2 players, the first duel appears right away.
  • Run the duel. When you’re done, tap Next player and pass the device.
  • Use Tap to reveal when it shows-category or letter comes from the app so neither dueler peeks early.

Duel (no neutral judge)

  1. The card explains the duel.
  2. You two run it yourselves-no third person needed for these duels.
  3. Agree on a winner, then tap Next player.

Judge-required duels are removed in 2-player mode. If you need a redo, use Swap on the duel card.

Optional scoring

Winner of each duel: +2 points. High score after 6 duels wins. Ties: run one more duel.
