How to play

33 Seconds of Chaos, Pass and Play App Rules

A fast party game where you follow ridiculous prompts, shout wild guesses, and sometimes end up in surprise head-to-head duels. One device. Pass it around.

Quick Start

  • Hand the device to the next player. Tap Start.
  • The app gives you either a Word turn or a Duel.
  • When you're done, tap Next Player and pass the device.

Word Turn

  1. Read the Chaos Prompt out loud.
  2. Keep the Word secret.
  3. Tap the timer to start 33 seconds.
  4. Give clues that follow the prompt and the Basic Rules.
  5. Everyone else shouts unlimited guesses.

When someone guesses the word:

  • Stop the timer if it's still running.
  • Reveal the word.
  • Tap Next Player.

If time runs out:

  • Reveal the word.
  • Tap Next Player.

Close enough counts. You decide if a guess counts. If it's close, give it to them and keep moving.

Duel

  1. The screen tells you who duels.
  2. Choose a Neutral Judge (someone not dueling).
  3. The Neutral Judge reads the duel out loud and runs it.
  4. Duel until the Neutral Judge declares a winner.
  5. Tap Next Player.

Judge's call is final.

Some duels (Alpha Blitz, Theme Blitz, Scavenge) hide key info until you tap Tap to reveal so nobody gets a head start.

Swap

If a prompt, word, or duel is a bad fit, tap Swap and confirm.

Basic Rules

Unless the prompt says otherwise, you may not:

  • Say any part of the word (including obvious root forms or plurals).
  • Spell it, write it, or trace letters.
  • Say "rhymes with."
  • Show the object or an image of it.

Any player can call a rule break. If it's unclear, vote fast, then move on.

Optional Scoring

If you want a winner, track points however you like.

Points

  • Word turn (guessed in time): clue-giver +1, first correct guesser +1
  • Word turn (no guess): nobody scores
  • Duel: winner +2

Rounds

Play 3 rounds (each player takes one turn per round). High score wins.

Ties

Run one more duel. Winner takes it.
