    How to Play

    33 Seconds of Chaos is a party game for 3-8 players that's easy to teach and wild to play. You draw Chaos cards that tell you how to give clues, and Word cards everyone tries to guess. Each round lasts 33 seconds, so turns stay fast, loud, and funny from start to finish.

    1. Draw a Chaos card to see how you're allowed to give clues.
    2. Draw a Word card and start the 33-second timer.
    3. Give clues using the Prompt on the Chaos card while everyone else shouts guesses.
    4. The first player to guess it scores with you. After 10 rounds, the highest score wins.

    TL;DR: Draw a Chaos card. Draw a word. Shout ridiculous clues for 33 seconds. Score points. Repeat.
